Fisherman's Birthday Celebration 

Let's Gogouyave- Celebrating our tradition and progress 

Fisherman Birthday is an event in the psyche of the people of Gouyave, from April, you will hear reference of “the June coming” with all the excitement in their voices and eyes. The people of Gouyave has no time for the first 28 days of June, they wish it could move from June 1st to 29th , they all look forward to the 29th. Its in way our NATIONAL DAY, our DAY TO SHOW OFF!!
We don’t know and cannot accurately say when the first Fisherman birthday festivities was organized. Every older person you asked, will tell you that since he/she was small, they could remember it. Somewhere in the late 60’s it went a notch higher with better organized event. During the rule of the GULP of Sir EM Gairy, in 1976, Gouyave and its fishing industry got a major shot in the arm with the installation of a large freezer and the converting of what was the senior section of the RC school into the Gouyave fish market. For those that knows the l’anse, the freezer was situated to the back of Ms Daphne house (opposite Kelly Hot spot)
However, it was during the reign of the PRG that the celebration got its significance with the half-holiday and some monetary contribution. The industry really kicked off with the help of the Cubans who had a different type of fishing method, long line. Then came the advent of the outboard engine and the fast pirogue but this is a different story that we will go into another time.
Every parish now has a “DAY” and am almost sure without Fisherman Birthday , (Gouyave day), they wouldn’t be any interest in that, (smile.).The template for other parish day is taken from us. However, we are different. While other event suffered over the years in varying degrees. There has NEVER been a lack luster fisherman birthday. Grenadian expect and knows that they will have a good time in Gouyave. Everyone want to be part of the Gouyave experience. Regardless what the Event planners organizes, the majority of the crowd comes at nighttime for the fun. No other festival can boast of that.
Significantly, Fisherman Birthday marketability is HUGE, nowhere in Grenada or the Caribbean, you can have an event like that on a WORKING day and get a crowd out. Unlike other communities, Gouyave and it environ can support and sustain any event that it has. We take a certain kind of pride in our own. Nothing in Gouyave will fail, nothing that is happening in Gouyave depends on outside crowd to make it great. Our energy is different.
Fisherman Birthday in Gouyave, is an unforgettable experience …. it’s an event, it our life, its Uniquely Gouyave.
It’s in we blood, Fishing, we’re proud.
