Fisherman's Birthday 

Fisherman birthday is a NATIONAL EVENT, while various fishing communities celebrate the event in their area, all eyes are on Gouyave, the Fishing Mecca. In 1980, it was declared a half holiday by the People Revolutionary Government(PRG).
The event dates back to the Roman Catholic Church. It’s the day of the patron Saint of the fisherman, St. Peter and St. Paul. From a historical perspective, the church and the rest of Grenada had always regarded Gouyave as the FISHING CAPITAL and thus Gouyave is also St. Peter’s parish in Grenada in the church configurations.
